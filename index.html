Cars

Mazda RX-7 FD

The Mazda RX-7 FD is a legendary JDM sports car that captivates enthusiasts with its sleek design and rotary engine. Featured in Initial D, this iconic vehicle showcases the perfect blend of style and performance.

Toyota AE86

The Toyota AE86, also known as the "Hachi-Roku" (Japanese for "eight-six"), is an iconic car that holds a special place in the hearts of Initial D fans. Driven by the series' protagonist, Takumi Fujiwara, this rear-wheel-drive coupe has become synonymous with drifting and mountain racing.

Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution III

The Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution III is a rally-bred powerhouse that commands respect on both the road and the track. Renowned for its turbocharged engine and all-wheel-drive system, this iconic JDM sedan delivers adrenaline-pumping performance in every drive.
